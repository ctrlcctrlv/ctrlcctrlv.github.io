Hellworld Dispatch

Dispatches🖹 from my corner of Hellworld🔥🌎, which is mostly online. These dispatches will never contain ads for for-profit corporations. This is an experiment in the lowest effort possible to exert and still have something able to be described as a “podcast”. Topics will vary greatly. It is very likely that if I do this for long enough one day I will be in the mood to deliver an entire thirty minute oration in Esperanto, much to your annoyance.
